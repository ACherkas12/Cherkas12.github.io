Some reasons to go to our training:

  • First of all - Ultimate is fun.
  • Secondly - Our sport has no physical contact.
  • Futhermore - We have a lot of tournaments all over the world (when it possible) and big parties after it.
  • One more reason - We are not toxic. Ultimate is the most kind sport in the world. It is so because we have the oficial spirit rules.
  • More than that - One day you can go to the Olimpic games. Ultimate is not popular sport, that is why there is no rivarly. It is your chance!
  • You need more advantages? No problem! - Ultimate is cheap. Do you have sportswear and a bottle of water? That's all what you need.
  • One more? Ok! - You are a girl or a boy? We do not care! Our team includes both girls and boys. Also you don't need to be a sports hunter. Ultimate is a wonderful combination both of serious sport and fun community. What is about age? Now our team's players are from 19 to 35 years old.

There is a special moment that I remember for a long time. It happened during the World Cup in the USA. It was a late afternoon, when the sun was approaching the horizon, Pavel Budnik and I were returning to the tent tired after the games. Food was distributed under it, the players ate and communicated with each other. At that time, a game was being played on a nearby field. I was impressed by the game of mixed teams by the fact that players enjoyed every second on the field. They were happy and smiling, supporting each other and rivals as if it was a friendly match of the best friends. At the same time, the game was super intense and tense until the last minutes. I couldn't believe it was already evening, and the players had so much energy that they could charge the whole tent with it instead of delicious food. "It seems that this is the real Ultimate," - I shared with Pavel.

- Belarusian national team player Alex Tarasenko
